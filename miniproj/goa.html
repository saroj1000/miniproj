Welcome to Goa

               The state of Goa in India is famous for its beaches and places of worship. Tourism is its primary industry, and is generally focused on the coastal areas of Goa, with decreased tourist activity inland. Goa's beaches cover about 125 kilometres (78 mi) of its coastline.
