"Travel Agency Management system"


                The main purpose of “Travel Agency Management System” is to provide a convenient way for a customer to book flight, train and bus for tour purposes. The objective of this project is to develop a system that automates the processes and activities of a travel agency.


                This application is develop to provide best travelling services to the customers and travel agents. We have developed Travel Agency Management System to provide a search platform where a tourist can find their tour places according to their choices. This system also helps to promote responsible and interesting tourism so that people can enjoy their holidays at their favorable places. We develop this system to create and promote forms of tourism that provide healthy interaction opportunities for tourists and locals and increase better understanding of different cultures, customs, lifestyles, traditional knowledge and believes.
