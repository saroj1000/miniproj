Welcome to Kashmir

                Kashmir is “Switzerland of India”. Kashmir is valley of flowers, enclosed by the snow-clad mountains, trekking trails and the gleaming lakes create a picturesque landscape on earth it is also known as Slice of heaven on Earth this reflects the nature’s beauty at its best. It is a best destination for leisurely family vacations, solo trip and trekking. Vaishno Devi is very famous for the holy shrine of the Hindu Mother Goddess located in these hills. Thousands of pilgrims, every year trek their way up the steep slopes, to offer prayer to their goddess. Vaishno Devi is located 61 km north of Jammu at a height of 5,200 feet above the sea level.
